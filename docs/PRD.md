# PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)

**EduFordge**
*Career Counselling / Career Guidance / Student Mentorship / Psychometric Assessment / College & Career Planning Platform*
**Version:** 1.0
**Prepared For:** EduFordge
**Document Type:** Product Requirements Document (PRD)
**Reference Inspiration:** PixelShield theme style, motion feel, section hierarchy, CTA presentation, and premium SaaS landing-page experience only.
**Product Domain:** Career Counselling / Student Guidance / Psychometric Assessment / Mentorship / College & Career Planning

## 1. Executive Summary
### 1.1 Product Name
EduFordge
### 1.2 Product Vision
EduFordge will be a modern Career Counselling and Student Success Platform that helps students, parents, schools, counsellors, and career mentors make better academic and career decisions through psychometric assessments, counselling sessions, career roadmaps, skill-gap analysis, college/course discovery, mentorship, and AI-assisted career guidance tools.
EduFordge should combine:
- Premium trust-building public website
- Lead generation + consultation booking
- Student self-discovery journey
- Psychometric testing engine
- Counsellor CRM / workflow dashboard
- Career planning and roadmap management
- School / institution partnership workflows
- Admin operations, CMS, analytics, and reporting

## 2. Design Direction & Product Experience
### 2.1 Design Inspiration Source
The EduFordge website UI/UX should take inspiration from the feel of PixelShield in the following ways:
**Use from inspiration**
- Strong hero-first landing page
- Premium dark/modern or high-contrast polished UI aesthetic
- Smooth scroll animations and section reveals
- Large CTA-driven blocks
- Rounded cards with layered spacing
- Trust-building section flow
- Service/module explanation cards
- Consultation / booking CTA sections
- Minimal but elegant gradients, glows, floating highlights, and transitions
- Productized service presentation style
- Sticky top navigation with clear CTA buttons
- Strong “platform + services” hybrid positioning

**Do NOT copy directly**
- Do not copy content, brand language, photography business messaging, icons, or structure 1:1.
- EduFordge must be a career-tech + counselling brand with its own identity.

## 4. Business Goals
EduFordge aims to:
- Generate leads for counselling and assessment services.
- Convert students/parents into paid counselling programs.
- Offer structured psychometric and career planning journeys.
- Enable counsellors to manage students, reports, sessions, and roadmaps.
- Offer school/institute partnership programs.
- Build recurring revenue via packages, subscriptions, assessments, and workshops.
- Provide scalable digital guidance using AI-assisted recommendations and knowledge content.
- Build brand authority in career counselling, admissions guidance, and mentoring.

## 9. Website UX & Visual Style Requirements
### 9.1 Visual Style
EduFordge should feel:
- Premium
- Modern
- High-conviction
- Professional but student-friendly
- Trust-led and action-led

### 9.2 PixelShield-inspired visual patterns to adopt
- Large hero headline with supporting sub-copy
- Dual CTA buttons
- Floating motion cards / subtle glow layers
- Smooth reveal on scroll
- Section blocks with strong spacing
- Card-based services and features
- Dark + vibrant accent or clean white + premium gradient theme
- Sticky navbar with CTA
- Trust badges / social proof rows
- FAQ accordion
- Consultation booking CTA bands
- Elegant footer with newsletter and quick links

### 9.3 Recommended EduFordge Theme Palette
**Option A: Dark Premium**
- Background: #0B1020 / #0F172A
- Primary Accent: #4F46E5
- Secondary Accent: #06B6D4
- Highlight Accent: #F59E0B
- Success Accent: #10B981
- Text Primary: #F8FAFC
- Text Secondary: #CBD5E1

**Option B: Light Premium**
- White / soft neutral background
- Indigo / Blue / Teal accents
- Gradient section highlights
- Deep slate headings

### 9.4 Animation Guidelines
Use animations inspired by the motion feel of PixelShield:
- Hero fade + slide-up reveal
- Staggered card appearance on scroll
- Hover elevation on service cards
- Number counters for stats
- Floating background shapes / blurred orbs
- Sticky CTA micro-interactions
- FAQ expand/collapse animations
- Step timeline reveal animations
- Dashboard preview panels with subtle parallax / motion
- Button ripple / glow hover effects
- Lottie / SVG line animations where relevant
- Avoid heavy animation that affects performance

## 32. Recommended Tech Stack
**Frontend**: Next.js / React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion
**Backend**: Node.js / NestJS / Express
**Database**: PostgreSQL
**Auth**: JWT + refresh token
**CMS**: Custom admin CMS
**Storage**: AWS S3
**Payments**: Razorpay / Stripe
**Analytics**: GA4

## 35. MVP Scope
**Phase 1 MVP**: Public website, Lead capture, Student/parent login, Counsellor dashboard basic, Assessment engine basic, Roadmap basic, payment packages, admin CMS, blog/resources.
**Phase 2**: college/course library full, school partner dashboard, AI assistant, advanced analytics.
